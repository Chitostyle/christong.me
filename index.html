Projects

Clio Payments

Fintech

Clio is the leading cloud-based legal practice managment system in the world.

Created an online payment processor supporting the unique needs of legal firms on a team of four designers. Clio Payments released late October, 2021 has already processed millions of dollars in payments volume.

Trust Reconciliation

Fintech + Compliance

Led the initiative to provide trust reconciliation in Clio to ensure client funds are safe while also providing firms the peace of mind that they will be paid for the work they do.

This is critical to firms as the penalties for non-compliance are severe, and may include suspension or disbarment.

QuickBooks Online

Fintech + Integration / API

Clio’s integration with QuickBooks Online brings together two leading software to provide law firms with a cohesive accounting solution.

The philosophy of my work into this integration is to centralize firm financials, enable legal-specific accounting needs and help firms avoid double-entry to save them time and avoid costly mistakes.

Dialpad Integration

VOIP + Technical

With the COVID lockdown hitting the world hard and by surprise, Clio recognized a need to help law firms continue to be able to connect & collaborate with their clients.

The Dialpad integration provided firms an easy way to continue communicating with clients while providing automation to capture case details and time spent for billing. This integration resulted in a 700% increase in logs created on avg with less than 1% of these logs being deleted.

Tracker Redesign

Enterprise + Scaling Customer Needs

The first major redesign for Tracker after years of iterative improvements, the decision to redesign came as a result of a need to make some major changes to the application’s core areas to help it scale with the needs of its users.

The new design focused on improving collaboration across teams and increasing visibility + coordination for stakeholders to help them move in concert towards the organization’s north star.

Workflow Exploration

High Complexity + Strategy

Not all projects result in production deliverables. One of the common themes as Tracker’s customers succeeded was the need to support product companies at scale.

The goal of this initiative was to develop deep customer empathy towards the challenges of these scaling organizations. After digging deep into this problem space, a roadmap was established that prioritized customer pain points that had a combination of the greatest intensity and frequency to deliver the highest value possible.

Blockers

Team Collaboration

The first project prioritized as a result of the Workflow Exploration was to create a way to help teams easily identify bottlenecks in their development process that prevented them from working on stories and delivering them.

The addtion of blockers allowed teams to identify not only when stories had blockers, but also react quickly and appropriately to them. The release of blockers helped teams complete stories with blockers on them 17% more quickly.
